How the programme works

How Does Tulika Dutta's Weight Management Program Work?

Tulika Dutta's weight management program works by finding the cause of the weight gain before prescribing any plan. It combines assessment, familiar food, and weekly follow-ups that adjust the plan as the body responds.

  1. 01

    First, find out why the weight is there

    Before Tulika writes a single meal, she looks at blood reports, thyroid and hormone markers, medical history, medication, sleep, stress, and daily routine. Skipping this step is why generic charts fail.

  2. 02

    Evidence-aware, not trend-based

    The plan follows current nutrition science and avoids trending diets, fat-burner claims, and supplements replacing balanced plates. The useful change is the one you can repeat safely.

  3. 03

    Built around the food you already eat

    A weight loss diet plan that deletes rice from a Bengali kitchen will not survive a week. Tulika builds plans around maach-bhaat, dal, roti, and seasonal shobji, adjusting portions and timing instead of banning family food.

  4. 04

    Personalised to your real life

    Tulika accounts for work schedule, cooking time, food budget, and what you genuinely enjoy eating. A plan that ignores those things is a plan most people abandon.

Who it is for

Who Should Consult a Weight Loss Nutritionist in Kolkata?

A weight loss nutritionist in Kolkata is worth consulting when the weight will not move despite effort, or when it is tied to a health condition. These cards show common starting points.

Lifestyle-related weight gain

Sedentary jobs, long screen hours, irregular meals, stress eating, and poor sleep drive a large share of adult weight gain. If your weight crept up over a few years of desk work and takeaways, this is a common starting point.

PCOS and PCOD-related weight gain

PCOS or PCOD can make weight harder to manage because of the hormone and insulin-resistance picture behind it. Tulika builds hormone-aware plans for this. PCOS-related weight management route pending.

Thyroid-related weight changes

An underactive thyroid can affect metabolism and weight. Tulika coordinates food timing and planning around reports and medication. Thyroid-related weight changes route pending.

Diabetic weight gain

Weight and blood sugar often move together, so diet structure can support insulin sensitivity alongside medical care. Tulika coordinates with your physician where diabetes treatment is active. Diabetic weight management route pending.

Visceral fat and fatty liver

Fat stored around the organs can carry metabolic risk and needs a practical food plan matched to reports, routine, and medical guidance. Fatty liver and visceral fat route pending.

Post-pregnancy weight management

Postpartum nutrition needs recovery-aware planning, and where relevant, breastfeeding-aware choices. Crash dieting is not the route here. Post-pregnancy weight management route pending.

Fat loss with muscle gain

For people already training but not seeing change, nutrition is often the missing piece. Tulika aligns food structure with training, recovery, and body-composition goals. Fat loss and muscle gain route pending.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions About Weight Management

Practical answers before you decide whether to start weight-management nutrition care with Tulika.

How much weight can I expect to lose per week?

A realistic pace depends on medical history, hormones, sleep, stress, medication, activity, and consistency. Tulika discusses expectations after assessment and does not promise a fixed weekly number.

Do I need to exercise to lose weight with your program?

Exercise can support health and body composition, but the food plan is built around your current capacity. If you already train, Tulika can align meals with recovery and routine.

Will I need to cook separate meals for my family?

No. The plan is designed to work with familiar family food where possible, using portion structure, timing, simple swaps, and balanced additions.

What if I have PCOS, thyroid, or diabetes along with weight issues?

Tulika accounts for medical history, reports, and current treatment while planning food. Where a condition needs active medical management, nutrition support should stay coordinated with your physician.

How is this different from a gym trainer's diet plan?

Tulika's work starts with nutrition assessment, medical context, food habits, and follow-up review. Training goals can be supported, but the plan is not limited to workout-day eating.

Can I follow the program if I am vegetarian?

Vegetarian food preferences can be discussed during consultation and planned around using familiar protein, fibre, and meal-balance options where appropriate for the person.

Nutrition basics

Nutrition Basics, Myths & FAQs

The questions people ask before they start: what a nutrition professional does, how to choose guidance, and why credentials should be checked carefully.

Nutritionist vs dietitian: what is the difference?

A dietitian usually has formal dietetics training and may be registered depending on the country or professional system. A nutritionist may hold professional nutrition training and certifications. Tulika Dutta uses the public credential language Certified Nutritionist & Dietitian and does not use registered-title claims.

What does a nutritionist actually do?

A nutritionist helps translate health goals, food habits, routine, reports, preferences, and follow-up needs into a practical eating plan. For health conditions, nutrition support should work alongside medical advice, not replace it.

How do I choose a good nutritionist?

Look for clear credential language, realistic guidance, transparent boundaries, no unrealistic outcome promises, and a plan that fits your actual life. Good guidance should explain why changes are being made, not just hand over a chart.

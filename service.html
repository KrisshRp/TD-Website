The approach

What Makes Tulika Dutta's Nutrition Programmes Different?

Most people who come to Tulika Dutta have already tried something: a supplement programme, a crash diet, or a photocopied calorie chart from a practitioner who never called back. Four things make her programmes different.

The cause is treated, not the symptom

Weight gain can be driven by insulin resistance, a thyroid imbalance, medication, or a broken sleep pattern. Tulika reviews blood reports, medical history, and daily routine before a meal is prescribed.

Plans are built from food you already eat

Programmes are built around maach-bhaat, dal, roti, seasonal vegetables, and the food already cooking in your kitchen. A plan you cannot cook is a plan you will not follow.

Weekly follow-ups, not a one-time chart

Tulika checks progress each week and adjusts the plan as life changes: a new work shift, a festival, a travel week, or a fresh lab report.

You are taught, not just told

Tulika explains the reasoning behind each change, from blood sugar and protein timing to what a lab value means, so the plan can hold together outside the consultation room.

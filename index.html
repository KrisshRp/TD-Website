FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions About Nutrition Consultations in Kolkata

Practical answers before you decide whether to start with WhatsApp, Serampore, Khardaha, or online consultation.

How much does a nutrition consultation in Kolkata cost?

Consultation fees must be confirmed directly with Tulika before publishing. The first WhatsApp conversation can clarify the consultation scope, location, and next step without adding unverified pricing to the page.

Do you offer online consultations outside Serampore and Khardaha?

Yes. In addition to in-person consultations at the Serampore and Khardaha chambers, Tulika offers online consultations via video call across India. Personalized meal plans and follow-up support can be delivered through WhatsApp and email.

Can a nutritionist help with PCOS and thyroid-related weight gain?

PCOS/PCOD and thyroid management are core nutrition support areas. Tulika uses individualized diet plans that consider insulin resistance, hormonal balance, familiar Indian foods, and weekly adjustments based on each person's response.

What is the difference between a nutritionist and a dietitian?

A dietitian typically holds a degree in dietetics and may be registered with a professional body. A nutritionist holds professional certifications in nutrition science. Tulika Dutta is a certified nutritionist and dietitian with diplomas in Nutrition and Dietetics from NFNA and IGMPI.

What happens in a first nutrition consultation?

The first session reviews body measurements, health history, eating patterns, lifestyle, and goals. Based on this assessment, Tulika creates a personalized meal plan tailored to routine, food preferences, and follow-up needs.

Do I need to cook separate meals for my family?

No. Meal plans are designed to be family-friendly where possible. Portion adjustments and simple swaps help familiar Bengali and Indian meals fit more naturally into daily home cooking.

How quickly will I see results?

Results vary by condition, consistency, metabolism, and the plan being followed. Tulika tracks progress through follow-ups and adjusts the plan without promising a fixed timeline or certain outcome.

Can vegetarians and vegans follow your programs?

Yes. Tulika can design vegetarian and vegan meal plans using plant-based protein sources such as lentils, legumes, soy, nuts, seeds, and other foods suited to the person's needs.
